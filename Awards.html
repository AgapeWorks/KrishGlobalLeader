Costa Book Awards

John Newbery Medal

To emphasize to the public that contributions to the literature for children deserve similar recognition to poetry, plays, or novels.

Edgar Award

honor the best in mystery fiction, non-fiction, television, film, and theater published or produced in the previous year.

Man Booker Prize

Each year, the prize is awarded to what is, in the opinion of the judges, the best sustained work of fiction written in English and published in the UK and Ireland. It is a prize that transforms the winner's career.

National Book Award

National Book Awards are given to one book (author) annually in each of five categories: fiction, nonfiction, poetry, translation, and young people's literature
