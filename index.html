About Me

Radhakrishnan Arikrishna Perumal is a globally recognized expert in artificial intelligence and digital transformation, specializing in the insurance industry. His work has set new industry benchmarks and influenced global best practices. Radhakrishnan Arikrishna Perumal's thought leadership extends beyond technical contributions. As a recipient of prestigious awards for innovation and excellence,Radhakrishnan has been recognized as a trailblazer in his field. His contributions have been featured in leading industry publications, further cementing his reputation as a transformative force in insurance technology.
